Lasagna

Description

Pasta dish with meat served between layers of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta cheese
  • sweet Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • parslet, dired basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seedes, black pepper
  • Lasagna noodeles
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna reset before serving
